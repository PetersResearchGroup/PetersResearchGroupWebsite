Welcome to the Peters Research Group!

Studying ordered soft-matter systems using simulation and experiment

We are working to facilitate nanosale control by applying the fundamentals of block copolymer phase separation and polymer dynamics.

A combination of advanced coarse-grained simulation methods and experimental techniques are used to understand and control ordered soft matter systems.

For more information, meet our members or check out our research projects and publications.
